BerryBelle

Every studio needs a tiny guardian.

Mine became BerryBelle: cheerful, pastel, curious, dramatic in a cute way, and always carrying sparkle.

She looks like a strawberry milk carton, but she also feels like a piece of my personality: sweet, soft, creative, and quietly bold.

I created her as a mascot, but sometimes I think she is also a softer version of me.
